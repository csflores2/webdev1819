Gus Giordano Dance School


The Gus Giordano Foundation is a non-profit organization focused on continuing Gus Giordano's mission of elevating dance by providing scholarships and education to youth, teens, and college dancers, to enrich their lives and inspire them to keep the art of dance alive.
