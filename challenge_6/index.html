THE ROTHSCHILDS

ABOUT

The Rothschild family is a wealthy Jewish family that descends from Mayer Amschel Rothschild (1744–1812). Amschel Rothschild, a court factor to the German Landgraves of Hesse-Kassel in the Frankfurt, and the Holy Roman Empire, established his banking business in the 1760s. Unlike most previous court factors, Rothschild managed to pass on his wealth and established an international banking family through his five sons, who established themselves in London, Paris, Frankfurt, Vienna, and Naples. The Rothschilds were elevated to noble rank in the Holy Roman Empire and the United Kingdom.
